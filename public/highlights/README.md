# Stream Highlights Videos

Place your portrait video files here with simple names:

- video1.mp4
- video2.mp4
- video3.mp4
- etc.

## Video Requirements:
- **Format**: MP4 (recommended)
- **Orientation**: Portrait (9:16 ratio)
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920 or 720x1280
- **File Size**: Keep under 50MB for best performance
